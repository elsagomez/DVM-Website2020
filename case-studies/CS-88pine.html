Wall Street Plaza - 88 Pine Street, NY

88 Pine Street is a Building located in the Financial District neighborhood in Manhattan, NY. 88 Pine Street was built in 1973 and has 32 stories and 23 units.

DVM installed a high performance UVC system and implemented the following components:

  • Installed 120 emitters and ballasts capable of 110V, 208V and 277V service. Emitters were installed perpendicular to the cooling coil.
  • Installed emitters produce high output UVC energy to eliminate biofilm, reduce or eliminate airborne microorganisms.
  • Installed ballast panels in NEMA 4 enclosure.
  • Installed UVC system control panel in NEMA 4 enclosure. The control panel carries safety features, such as UL listed, door-mounted disconnect switch and a front-panel reset button.
  • Install viewport assembly with safety glass.
  • Install new access doors for units that require them
